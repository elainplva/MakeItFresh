Potato and Cheese

This is a simple yet very delcious receipe that you can experiment with different flavours.


Cook Time: 40 minutes

Top Tips

  • You can customize this dish by adding cooked bacon, ham, or chopped vegetables like broccoli or bell peppers between the layers for added flavor and texture.
  • Make sure to slice the potatoes thinly and evenly to ensure even cooking.
  • To save time, you can use pre-shredded cheese and skip the step of making the cheese sauce from scratch. Simply layer the sliced potatoes with the shredded cheese and bake as directed.

Equipment

  • Large pot
  • Colander
  • Mixing bowl
  • Baking dish
  • Oven

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped green onions, cooked bacon bits, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Potatoes:
    Place the sliced potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low and let the potatoes simmer for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are just tender. Be careful not to overcook them. Drain the potatoes in a colander and set them aside.
  3. Make Cheese Sauce:
    In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour to create a roux. Cook the roux for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Cook the sauce until it thickens and becomes smooth, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese until it melts and the sauce is creamy. Season the cheese sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Layer Potatoes and Cheese:Arrange half of the sliced potatoes in the bottom of a greased baking dish. Pour half of the cheese sauce over the potatoes, spreading it evenly.
  5. Bake:
    Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Serve:
    Remove the foil from the baking dish and let the potato and cheese dish cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped green onions, cooked bacon bits, or sour cream if desired.
