CheeseCake

This homemade no bake cheesecake recipe will leave you drooling with its freach flavours


Cook Time: 23 minutes

Top Tips

  • For best results, ensure that the cream cheese is softened at room temperature before using to prevent lumps in the cheesecake filling.
  • If you prefer a sweeter crust, you can add a little more sugar to the graham cracker crust mixture.
  • To make slicing easier, dip a sharp knife in hot water and wipe it dry before slicing the cheesecake. Repeat this process between each slice.

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Spatula
  • 9-inch springform pan
  • Food processor or rolling pin (for crushing graham crackers)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Refrigerator

Ingredients

    For the Crust:
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (about 10-12 graham crackers)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • For the Cheesecake Filling:
  • 16 oz (450g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust:
    If using whole graham crackers, crush them into fine crumbs using a food processor or by placing them in a plastic bag and rolling over them with a rolling pin.
    In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix until well combined and the mixture resembles wet sand.
  2. Press Crust Into Pan:
    Press the graham cracker mixture evenly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan, using the back of a spoon or a flat-bottomed glass to compact it firmly. Ensure that the crust is evenly distributed.
  3. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:
    In a separate mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese with an electric mixer or whisk until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add Sugar and Vanilla:
    Gradually add the granulated sugar and vanilla extract to the cream cheese mixture, beating until well combined and smooth.
  5. Whip Heavy Cream:
    In another mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form using an electric mixer.
  6. Fold in Whipped Cream:
    Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until fully incorporated. Be careful not to deflate the whipped cream.
  7. Assemble the Cheesecake:
    Pour the cheesecake filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.
  8. Chill the Cheesecake:
    Cover the springform pan with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow it to set and firm up.
  9. Serve and Enjoy:
    Once the cheesecake is fully chilled and set, carefully remove the sides of the springform pan. Slice the cheesecake into servings and serve chilled. Optionally, garnish with fresh fruit, whipped cream, or a drizzle of chocolate sauce before serving.
